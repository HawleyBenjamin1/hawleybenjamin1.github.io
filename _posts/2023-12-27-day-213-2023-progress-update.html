It's almost New Year's, and that means that this will be the last progress update of 2023. It's been a fantastic year for me, one where I've been able to follow my dreams further than ever before. I've completed and edited several short stories (five, I believe), finished off the first draft of my first novel, started on a second, and even accomplished a long time dream of getting something published; my poem 'Mayflies' which you can find in this anthology if you missed it. I couldn't have asked for more out of 2023.

Next year I hope the trend continues. My goal will be to get something published again, a story if possible. A novel if I'm incredibly lucky. Maybe I will try self publishing a story or two while I'm at it. Of course I will keep up with the blog, since it has been such a useful tool in keeping me motivated and focused throughout these past ten months.
