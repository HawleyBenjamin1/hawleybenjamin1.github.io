Not so long ago an interesting idea floated across my screen in the form of this image:

These were focused on romance tropes, but it got me thinking about other genres and some that might have interesting tropes reverse. Of course my mind went to sci-fi first, so here I've compiled a little list of the best ones I could come up with. I think I might write out a few of these for fun:

  • Resetting the polarity to normal.

  • Alien invasion where the aliens are the ones that are technologically outmatched.

  • Slower than light travel is hard.

  • The hivemind gets more dangerous when the queen goes down.

  • A dystopian world where everyone is fighting to make things worse.

  • A spaceship built in secret that really, really sucks.

  • Exosuits that are actually living beings, hiding a vulnerable machine core.

  • Way too much of a rare material.

  • The star ship's handsome Captain is notoriously bad with women.

  • Reach in with your feelings.

If you've got any more, send em' along! I'd love to brainstorm some of these for fantasy too.
