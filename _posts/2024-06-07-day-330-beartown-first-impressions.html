I picked up the first book in a trilogy called 'Beartown' by Fredrik Backman for an upcoming book club I'll be attending soon. It centers on a small town hockey team with dreams of winning nationals, I believe in Norway, where the author is from. I'm not sure what to think yet exactly. This isn't my usual kind of novel, but hey, I joined the book club so I could get exposed to some new stuff. The style is ... interesting. I read one reviewer who described it as 'lyrical,' though I'm not really sure how well that applies. Once I wrap my head around it a little better I'll be able to give a better synopsis. The opening is strong though, with a surprisingly hooking intro. I'm excited to read the rest of this one and see how it goes. Not sure if I'll ever get to the next two books though.

Thank you for reading,
