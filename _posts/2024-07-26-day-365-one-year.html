365 days of Dangerous Day Job!

Thank you to everyone who read the blog at some point along the line. I'm not sure I would have kept up without your support. It's been such a valuable routine for me and I'm glad there's at least a few people out there to keep me honest. I've learned a lot this past year, and much of it has been while writing for this very page alone. Literary techniques, thematic elements, the history of fictional technology, and so much more! I refined my reviews, I stretched my brain researching and writing articles, and even put up a few stories I felt good about. This has been a wonderful experience and I'm glad I took on the challenge of keeping up with a blog. Some days it's been great, and some days it's been the last thing I wanted to do, but I'm proud to say I made it 365 days without missing a single post. I don't think I've ever done anything that regularly, that long, without missing it at least a time or two. All in all, I'm happy to have this blog to my name.

Here's to the past year of posts, and to the next one after that!

Thank you so much for reading,

Benjamin Hawley
