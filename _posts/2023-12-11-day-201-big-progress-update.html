I have great news! I finally finished the first draft of my novel today! After writing like 4000 words in a day, I finally finished it off in one big burst. It feels really weird to be honest. More of a relief than anything. Not finishing even a first draft would have been a huge letdown, so at least that possibility is gone. I have a couple of holes to fill in during editing, and a bunch of stuff to rewrite cause I know it's gonna hurt to read it back, but yay! I did a thing! I even kinda like the ending I wrote, and wrapped up more than I thought I would be able to with some last minute inspiration. I can't wait to let it sit around for a couple of months while I do something else, lol.

Huge thanks to all the people who've followed the blog all this time, it's been a big help in keeping me on track (even if I did finish four and a half months later than I wanted to, but you didn't hear that from me). I'm sure it would have taken a lot longer without anybody to report back to, warm up with, or give exciting news like this to.

As always, thank you so much for reading,

Benjamin Hawley
