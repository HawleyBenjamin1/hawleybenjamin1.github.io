We just got the first several inches of snow fall here in Raleigh that started this afternoon. It's supposed to continue until 1 PM tomorrow. I've never seen this much snow here before. Other than it making driving a lot more nerve wracking, it's pretty magical to see everything in a fluffy layer. I almost got lost driving around town because it was so unfamiliar. I guess I'll be spending the next few days at home, watching the snow fall and reading a good book, and hopefully writing one too.
