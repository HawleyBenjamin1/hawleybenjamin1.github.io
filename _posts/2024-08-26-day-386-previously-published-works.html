Today I learned that there are all kinds of competitions and publications that also accept previously published works. I mean I knew that already. But I didn't realize there were so many! I mention this because there is a big poetry contest I entered last year that will accept previously published poems. Of course I didn't win last year, but hey, I may as well enter with a poem I know is publishing quality already, right?
